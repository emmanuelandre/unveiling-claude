# **Product Requirement Document (PRD)**

**Product Name:** PromptOps
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** Product Management Team

---

## **1. Product Overview**

**PromptOps** is an AI-first platform designed to help enterprises and teams **create, optimize, and manage AI prompts at scale**. It provides a collaborative environment for prompt engineering, workflow orchestration, and performance analytics, enabling businesses to accelerate AI adoption while maintaining governance and cost control.

---

## **2. Target Users**

### **Primary Users**

- **AI Engineers & Developers:** Need a structured environment to experiment with prompts and integrate them into applications.
- **Data Scientists:** Require tools to evaluate prompt performance and optimize for accuracy and cost.
- **Product Managers:** Want visibility into AI usage, costs, and performance metrics.
- **Enterprise IT Teams:** Need governance, compliance, and scalability for AI workloads.

### **Secondary Users**

- **AI Consultants & Agencies:** Use the platform to deliver prompt engineering services to clients.
- **Educators & Trainers:** Provide hands-on AI training using real-world workflows.

---

## **3. Business Objectives**

- **Accelerate AI Adoption:** Reduce time-to-market for AI features by streamlining prompt engineering.
- **Cost Optimization:** Track token usage and model performance to minimize unnecessary spend.
- **Governance & Compliance:** Ensure secure, auditable AI workflows for regulated industries.
- **Collaboration & Knowledge Sharing:** Enable teams to share best practices and version prompts.
- **Market Differentiation:** Position as an enterprise-grade, AI-first platform with scalability and observability.

---

## **4. Key Business Value**

- **Standardization:** Provides a unified platform for prompt engineering across teams.
- **Scalability:** Kubernetes-based deployment ensures elastic scaling for AI workloads.
- **Risk Reduction:** Built-in audit logs and RBAC reduce compliance risks.
- **Innovation Enablement:** Advanced features like A/B testing and workflow orchestration foster experimentation.

---

## **5. Market Segment**

- **Enterprise AI Teams** in finance, healthcare, retail, and manufacturing.
- **AI Consulting Firms** offering prompt optimization services.
- **AI Startups** building multi-model applications.
- **Education & Training Providers** for AI courses.

---

## **6. User Needs & Use Cases**

### **Core Use Cases**

1. **Prompt Creation & Testing**
   - Users need an intuitive interface to craft and test prompts with different models.
2. **Workflow Orchestration**
   - Users want to build multi-step AI workflows using LangGraph agents.
3. **Performance Evaluation**
   - Users need metrics like accuracy, latency, and cost for each prompt.
4. **Collaboration**
   - Teams require shared workspaces, version control, and role-based access.
5. **Governance**
   - Enterprises need audit logs, compliance checks, and secure deployments.

---

## **7. Success Metrics**

- **User Adoption:** Number of active enterprise accounts and teams.
- **Prompt Performance:** Reduction in prompt failure rates.
- **Cost Savings:** Percentage reduction in token usage per workflow.
- **Engagement:** Frequency of prompt versioning and A/B testing.
- **Revenue:** Subscription growth and enterprise contracts.

---

## **8. Constraints**

- Must comply with **data privacy regulations** (GDPR, SOC2).
- Must support **multi-tenancy** for enterprise customers.
- Must integrate with **existing AI APIs** (OpenAI, HuggingFace, custom models).
